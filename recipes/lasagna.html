Lasagna Recipe

Homepage

Recipe for a simple but delicious and of course, cheesy lasagna!

Difficulty = Easy

What do we need?

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Tomato Beef Ragu Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese

Let's begin!

Steps

  1. Fry the mince beef with some onions and garlic
  2. Once browned, add in tomato sauce to create the Ragu
  3. Let simmer for 30 minutes for taste to infuse
  4. Lay your lasagna sheets on a baking dish
  5. Beat eggs and brush lasagna sheet with egg wash
  6. Spread the Ragu evenly across the sheet layer
  7. Sprinkle grated cheddar and Mozarella on top
  8. Add another layer of Lasagna sheets and repeat Step 5 - 7 again until dish is full
  9. Cover the last lasagna sheet with Ricotta and remaining cheddar
  10. Place in oven for around 20 minutes
