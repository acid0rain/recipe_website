Chicken Katsu Curry

Recipe for a delicious crispy and golden Chicken Katsu Cury

Difficulty = Easy

Ingredients:

    Katsu Chicken

  • Chicken Breaast
  • 4 Eggs
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • 'Golden Curry' sauce cubes

    • Garlic Rice

  • Basmatic or Jasmine Rice
  • Butter
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. In 3 bowls prepare: flour, Beaten egg and breadcrumbs
  2. Cover the chicken in the flour, then the eggs and finally the breadcrumbs, then leave to rest
  3. In a pan, sautee your garlic and bay leaf in some butter
  4. Add rice in to the pan and stir
  5. Add water and salt and cook rice as per packet instructions
  6. In another pan, add in your sauce Cubes and desired water ammount and cook on low heat
  7. Heat up vegetable oil in a large frying pan
  8. Once oil is smoking, fry the breadcrumbed chicken
  9. Once rice, sauce and chicken are done. Serve them up together!
