Cottage Pie Recipe

Homepage

Recipe for a home comfort classic - the cottage pie

Difficulty = Medium

Let's Begin!

Ingredients:

  • Beef Mince
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery Sticks
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Beef Stock
  • Red Wine
  • Potatoes
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Fry mince until brown then remove from pan
  2. Add half the butter to pan and then add chopped onions, garlic, carrots and celery and fry until soft
  3. Deglaze the pan with the red wine and let alcohol evaporate
  4. Add your cooked mince
  5. Add your seasoning to taste
  6. Add beef stock and leave to simmer on low heat
  7. Part boil the potatoes, then remove and mash them
  8. Add butter and seasoning to the mash potato as per taste
  9. In an oven dish, lay your veggies and mince and then spread mash on top evenly
  10. Top it off with cheese and bake in the oven for 15 minutes until top is crispy!
